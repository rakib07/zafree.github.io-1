The brief

I know you are busy, so I’ll get straight to the point. I am a UX Engineer based in Dhaka. I use brain and fancy machines to create brands, products and experiences.

That’s more or less about me. But if you’re still curious, keep scrolling.

My Standard

Work is not meaningful unless we have a standard and we stand by it. Therefore, I have a few standards too. They keep me motivated for everything I do. They are there to protect me from drifting away from my path.

Impossible? I’m on it

I don’t wanna say that the word “impossible” doesn’t exist in my vocabulary. But I do love to take on new challenges.

Think mobile-first

The universe is big, but technically, we can grab it with our hands. These mobile devices now drive more than 80% traffic. Thats why I alaways think mobile-first.

Make it simple

It took me a while to realize that people love simple things and hate complexities. Ever since I understood that I started building simple things.

What I do?

I build relationships

I help you make simple but better products. And I think I’m pretty good at it.

I spent a lot of time thinking about myself. What is it that I actually do? Do I design websites? Yes, I do. Do I design apps and other digital products? Yup, I’ve made a handful of those. Do I create brands? Yeah, that too. Do I code? Definitely, I love CSS.

But that doesn’t really explain me.

After thinking and thinking, lying in my bed, and talking to my wife, I realized that I really hate problems. That’s why I don’t wanna face any. If I do, I try to solve it. When I’m out of problems, I help others to solve theirs. If you have any, hire me. We will work together to solve yours.

When you work with me, you’ll find me snoozing a lot, because it helps me think clearly, wisely. Then I wake up and start building a strategy that tells a story. I like breaking the problem into little pieces. My favourite programming language was Assembly. So you can imagine how efficiently I can break down the steps into smaller pieces. Finally, I join the pieces together when I’m done. And voila! The design is ready!

In short, I help you make simple but better products that your customers either love or hate to use.

Ok, got it.

But what do I actually do?

I design beautiful and functional digital products

Technology is shaping the modern world. More and more companies are popping up every day. Everyone is in a lifelong competition to come up with aesthetic designs.

When making beautiful software is the prime concern, functionality is the last thing that comes to mind. While product owners celebrate a beautiful design, users pay the price. That’s the philosophy I’m against.

Solving problems is my first priority. I try to make software more human. My aim is to build digital products that improve people's lives, make communication easier, and solve problems faster.
